COMPANY PROFILE

NATIONWIDE ERECTORS started operations in 1990 engaging in equipment trading, equipment rental and providing construction services to major contractors. In 1996, we expanded our construction services and was incorporated as NATIONWIDE ERECTORS CORPORATION with the objective of becoming a major force in the equipment intensive sector of the construction industry in the Philippines.

 

In 2001, we established NATIONWIDE CONTRACTING LLC in Dubai UAE that specializes in Horizontal Directional Drilling Trenchless Technology that involves Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD), Thrust Piping / Pipe Jacking, Pipe Ramming and associated Civil Works.

 

Today, the NATIONWIDE ERECTORS CORPORATION (NECO) grew it’s operations to strengthen its presence in the construction industry. Our expanded services include bored piling and foundations, steelworks fabrication and erection that includes pipe rolling for pipes up to 25 mm. thick and horizontal and vertical construction.
